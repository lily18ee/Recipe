Ingredients


  • Puris (readily available or homemade)
  • Mint-coriander chutney
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Boiled and mashed potatoes
  • Cooked black gram
    • (kala chana)
  • Chopped onions
  • Chaat masala
  • Sev (crispy chickpea noodles)

Instructions

  1. Take a Puri.
  2. Make a small hole in the top.
  3. Stuff it with mashed potatoes and cooked black gram.
  4. Add some chopped onions.
  5. Drizzle mint-corainder chutney and tamarind chutney.
  6. Sprinkle chaat masala.
  7. Top with sev.
  8. Eat immediately.

Enjoy your Panipuri!
