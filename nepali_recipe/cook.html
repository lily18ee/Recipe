MOMO recipe:


  • Ingredients of Chicken Momos
  • 6 servings
  • 450gm all purpose flour
  • 1 tablesoon refined oil
  • 2 large onion
  • 1 inch ginger
  • water
  • garlic paste
  • 300gm boiled chicken
  • 5 green chilli
  • salt
  • 2 teaspoon soy sauce
  • black pepper
  • 1/2 cup red bell pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the ground chicken, chopped onions, minced garlic, minced ginger, chopped cilantro, soy sauce, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Take a momo wrapper and place a spoonful of the chicken mixture in the center.
  3. Fold the wrapper over the filling, forming a half-moon shape. Pinch the edges to seal the momo.
  4. Repeat the process with the remaining wrappers and filling.
  5. Heat water in a steamer. Grease the steamer tray to prevent sticking.
  6. Place the momo on the greased tray, leaving some space between them.
  7. Steam the momo for about 15-20 minutes or until the wrappers are cooked through.
  8. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce, such as soy sauce with chili and garlic.

Enjoy your homemade chicken momo!
